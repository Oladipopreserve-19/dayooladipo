About me

Oladipo Dayo George is a PhD candidate in Environmental Engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, focused on water treatment and environmental remediation. He received his MSc in Soil and Environmental Sciences from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, researching soil nitrate leaching and greenhouse gas emissions in organic amended soils. Oladipo has received awards including the International Arctic Winter School Scholarship and the Cornell Climate Fellowship. In addition to his academic pursuits, Oladipo is active in his community as a member of the Uncle Sam Toastmasters Club and mentor at BootCamp X.
